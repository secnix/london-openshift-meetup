OpenShift Commons Gathering


Where users, partners, customers, contributors and upstream project leads come together to collaborate and work together on OpenShift.


APPLY TO BE A SPONSOR

The OpenShift Commons Gathering brings together experts from all over the world to discuss container technologies, best practices for cloud native application developers and the open source software projects that underpin the OpenShift ecosystem. The London event will gather developers, devops professionals and sysadmins together to explore the next steps in making container technologies successful and secure.
